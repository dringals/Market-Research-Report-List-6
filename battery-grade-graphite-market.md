<p><p>The battery grade graphite market consists of three main types: crystalline flake graphite, amorphous graphite, and vein graphite (lump). Crystalline flake graphite is known for its high purity and conductivity, making it suitable for lithium-ion batteries. Amorphous graphite, with a less structured form, offers versatility in various applications but generally has lower performance. Vein graphite, or lump graphite, is rare and highly valued for its high purity and natural crystalline structure, providing exceptional electrochemical properties for advanced battery technologies.</p></p>
