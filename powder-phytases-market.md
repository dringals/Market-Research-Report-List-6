<p><ul><li>Acid Phytase</li><li>Alkaline Phytase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder phytase market is primarily divided into two types: acid phytase and alkaline phytase. Acid phytase is effective in low pH environments, making it suitable for animal feed, particularly for poultry and swine, enhancing phosphorus availability during digestion. Alkaline phytase, on the other hand, operates optimally in higher pH conditions, beneficial for ruminants and certain plant-based applications. Each type plays a crucial role in improving nutrient absorption, reducing feed costs, and minimizing environmental phosphate pollution.</p></p>
