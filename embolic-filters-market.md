<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Embolic Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upper Chamber Embolic Filter</li><li>Inferior Chamber Embolic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embolic filters are medical devices designed to capture and prevent emboli from causing occlusions during cardiovascular procedures. The Upper Chamber Embolic Filter targets the heart’s upper chambers, effectively trapping clots that could lead to strokes. Conversely, the Inferior Chamber Embolic Filter is utilized in the lower chambers, primarily safeguarding against emboli traveling to vital organs. Together, these filters enhance patient safety during interventions like atrial fibrillation treatments and minimally invasive surgeries by minimizing the risk of embolic complications.</p></p>
