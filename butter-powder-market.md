<p><p>The butter powder market is primarily divided into two types: salted and unsalted butter powder. Salted butter powder contains added salt, offering a savory flavor ideal for seasoning, baking, and enhancing dishes. It is commonly used in savory recipes, snacks, and sauces. Unsalted butter powder, on the other hand, is preferred for baking and cooking where precise control over salt content is desired, allowing for versatility in sweet and savory applications. Both types provide convenience and longer shelf life.</p></p>
