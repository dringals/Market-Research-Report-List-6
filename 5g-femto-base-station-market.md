<p><p>The 5G Femto Base Station market includes two primary types: Stand Alone (SA) and Non-Stand Alone (NSA). SA architecture operates independently, leveraging a cloud-native core, allowing for full 5G capabilities without reliance on 4G infrastructure. In contrast, NSA combines existing 4G LTE networks with 5G technology, enabling a quicker rollout while leveraging current investments. While SA provides enhanced performance and lower latency, NSA offers immediate benefits and easier integration for operators transitioning to 5G. Together, they shape diverse deployment strategies.</p></p>
