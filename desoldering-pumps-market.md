<p><p>The desoldering pumps market primarily consists of two types: plunger type and bulb type. Plunger type pumps utilize a spring-loaded mechanism that allows users to create a vacuum when activated, efficiently removing melted solder from circuit boards. Bulb type pumps feature a squeeze bulb that generates suction when released, making them user-friendly and effective for small tasks. Both types cater to the needs of electronics repair and assembly, with varying designs to suit professional and hobbyist applications.</p></p>
