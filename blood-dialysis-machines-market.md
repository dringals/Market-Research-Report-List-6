<p><p>Blood dialysis machines are crucial for patients with kidney failure, facilitating waste removal from the blood. Hemodialysis uses a dialyzer to filter blood outside the body, while peritoneal dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdominal cavity as a filter. Hemofiltration involves continuous removal of excess fluid and toxins through a filtration process. Other market types may include specialized dialysis techniques or portable systems, catering to diverse patient needs and enhancing treatment accessibility. Each method offers distinct advantages based on clinical requirements.</p></p>
