<p><p>The automotive cylinder head gasket market comprises several types: Multi-Layer Steel (MLS), Solid Copper, Composite, and Elastomeric gaskets. MLS gaskets are durable, providing high sealing performance for modern engines. Solid Copper gaskets offer excellent thermal conductivity, suitable for high-performance applications. Composite gaskets combine materials for balanced performance but may require more frequent replacement. Elastomeric gaskets are flexible and effective for lower-pressure applications, often used in older vehicles. Each type caters to specific engine requirements and performance characteristics.</p></p>
