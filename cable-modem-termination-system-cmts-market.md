<p><p>A Cable Modem Termination System (CMTS) is a critical component in cable broadband networks that connects cable modem users to the internet. The market for CMTS includes two main types: Integrated CMTS (I-CMTS) and Modular CMTS (M-CMTS). Integrated CMTS combines all functionalities into a single device, offering simplicity and compactness. In contrast, Modular CMTS separates functions into distinct modules, providing scalability and flexibility for operators to upgrade specific components as needed, catering to varying network demands efficiently.</p></p>
