<p><p>The functional food additives market comprises various types, each serving distinct purposes. Sweeteners enhance flavor without adding calories, making them popular for health-conscious consumers. Preservatives extend shelf life by preventing spoilage and maintaining food quality. Emulsifiers help blend ingredients that typically don't mix, enhancing texture and stability in products. Thickeners improve the consistency and mouthfeel of food items, while other additives may include flavor enhancers and colorants, broadening the appeal and functionality of processed foods in the market.</p></p>
