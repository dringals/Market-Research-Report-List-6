Reaction Bonded Silicon Carbide combines a silicon infiltrant with pre-formed SiC for improved toughness. Hot Pressing Silicon Carbide utilizes heat and pressure to enhance phase density and properties. CVD Silicon Carbide is produced through chemical vapor deposition for high purity and uniformity. Other methods may include innovative techniques catering to specific industrial or technological needs.</p></p>
