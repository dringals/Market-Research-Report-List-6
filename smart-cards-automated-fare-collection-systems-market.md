<p><p>Smart card automated fare collection systems streamline public transport payments through various market types. Fareboxes are onboard devices that accept smart card payments, allowing for quick boarding. Ticket Vending Machines (TVMs) enable passengers to purchase and reload smart cards or buy tickets at stations. Validators are installed at entry and exit points, ensuring smooth passenger flow by confirming valid smart card usage. Together, these components enhance efficiency, reduce cash handling, and improve the overall travel experience for users.</p></p>
