<p><strong>The Portable Exchange Deionization Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Cation Exchange Resin</li><li>Anion Exchange Resin</li><li>Mixed Bed Resin </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Exchange Deionization (PEDI) Service market includes three key types of resins: cation exchange resin, anion exchange resin, and mixed bed resin. Cation exchange resins remove positively charged ions, effectively softening water and reducing hardness. Anion exchange resins target negatively charged ions, effectively eliminating contaminants like nitrates and sulfates. Mixed bed resins combine both cation and anion resins in a single vessel, providing comprehensive deionization for high-purity water applications. This versatility caters to various industries requiring deionization solutions.</p></p>
