<p><p>The flexible type epoxy resins market comprises three main types: urethane modified, rubber modified, and dimer acid epoxy resins. Urethane modified resins enhance flexibility and adhesion, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Rubber modified resins improve impact resistance and elasticity, ideal for coatings and adhesives. Dimer acid epoxy resins provide excellent chemical resistance and flexibility, often utilized in protective coatings and sealants. Each type offers distinct properties to meet diverse performance requirements in various industries.</p></p>
