<p><p>The **Plastics Processing Machinery Market** includes various types of equipment used to shape and form plastic materials. **Injection Molding Machines (IMM)** melt plastic and inject it into molds to create precise, complex parts. **Extrusion Machines** push molten plastic through a die to produce continuous shapes like pipes, sheets, or films. **Blow Molding Machines (BMM)** inflate heated plastic into hollow shapes, such as bottles or containers. The **Other** category encompasses specialized machinery like thermoforming, rotational molding, or compression molding for unique applications.</p></p>
