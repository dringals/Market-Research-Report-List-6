<p><ul><li>Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode(AMOLED) Dispaly</li><li>Passive Matrix OLED(PMOLED) Display</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smartphone OLED display market primarily consists of two types: Active-Matrix Organic Light-Emitting Diode (AMOLED) and Passive Matrix OLED (PMOLED) displays. AMOLED displays use a matrix of pixels that are individually controlled, allowing for higher resolutions, faster refresh rates, and superior color accuracy, making them popular in high-end smartphones. In contrast, PMOLED displays are simpler with a fixed pattern of pixels, offering lower resolution and functionality, typically used in smaller, less demanding applications like wearables or secondary displays.</p></p>
