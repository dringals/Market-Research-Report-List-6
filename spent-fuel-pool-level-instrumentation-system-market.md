<p><p>The Spent Fuel Pool Level Instrumentation System market includes several key technologies. Guided Wave Radar Systems use radar pulses to measure liquid levels based on time-of-flight. Through-Air Radar Systems transmit radar waves through the air to detect liquid levels, offering versatility in measurement. Bubbler Systems utilize air bubbles released at the pool's bottom to gauge water level by measuring pressure changes. Additionally, "Others" encompass alternative measurement technologies and innovative solutions tailored for specific application needs in spent fuel management.</p></p>
