<p><strong>The Dental Bone Graft Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Xenograft</li><li>Synthetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental bone graft materials market consists of three primary types: allografts, xenografts, and synthetic materials. Allografts are derived from human bone, providing a biocompatible scaffold that integrates well with existing tissue. Xenografts are sourced from animal bones, typically bovine, offering similar structural benefits while raising some biocompatibility concerns. Synthetic materials, such as hydroxyapatite or calcium phosphates, are engineered to mimic natural bone, ensuring consistent quality and minimizing disease transmission risks. Each type serves distinct clinical needs in dental procedures.</p></p>
