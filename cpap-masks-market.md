<p><p>The CPAP masks market comprises three primary types: nasal pillow masks, nasal masks, and full-face masks. Nasal pillow masks deliver air through small cushions that fit into the nostrils, offering minimal contact and a lightweight design, ideal for those who feel claustrophobic. Nasal masks cover the nose and provide a secure fit, suitable for varying sleep positions. Full-face masks encompass both the nose and mouth, making them effective for users who breathe through their mouths, enhancing comfort and compliance with therapy.</p></p>
