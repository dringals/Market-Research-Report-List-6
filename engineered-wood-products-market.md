<p><p>Engineered wood products encompass a variety of types, each designed for specific structural applications. Laminated Veneer Lumber (LVL) is made from bonded layers of thin wood, offering high strength for beams and headers. Structural wood I-beams combine an I-shaped configuration with lightweight wood for enhanced load-bearing capabilities. Glued Laminated Timber (glulam) consists of layers of wood bonded together, ideal for arches and long spans. Other products include plywood and oriented strand board (OSB), providing versatile construction solutions.</p></p>
