<p><p>The flame retardant adhesives market is primarily categorized into two types: halogen-free and halogen-based flame retardant adhesives. Halogen-free flame retardant adhesives are formulated without halogen elements, making them safer for health and the environment while providing effective fire resistance. In contrast, halogen flame retardant adhesives contain halogen compounds, offering high flame retardancy but raising concerns regarding toxicity and environmental impact during combustion. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, automotive, and construction, prioritizing safety and performance.</p></p>
