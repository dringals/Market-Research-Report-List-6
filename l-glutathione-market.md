<p><p>The L-Glutathione market is primarily categorized into two types: reduced and oxidized forms. Reduced glutathione (GSH) is the active form, known for its antioxidant properties, playing a crucial role in cellular protection and detoxification. Conversely, oxidized glutathione (GSSG) is the inactive form, resulting from the oxidation of GSH. The demand for reduced glutathione is higher in health supplements and cosmetics due to its efficacy in promoting skin health and enhancing immune function, while oxidized glutathione is mainly utilized in research and clinical applications.</p></p>
