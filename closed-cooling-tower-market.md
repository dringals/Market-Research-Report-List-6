<p><p>Closed cooling towers are specialized cooling systems that combine the principles of evaporative cooling and closed-loop systems. Unlike traditional open cooling towers, they utilize a heat exchanger to transfer heat from the process water to the ambient environment while minimizing water loss and contamination. This design makes them highly efficient and suitable for applications in industries such as power generation, manufacturing, and HVAC.</p><p>The Closed Cooling Tower Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.8% during the forecast period.
