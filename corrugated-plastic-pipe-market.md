<p><p>The corrugated plastic pipe market primarily consists of single wall and double wall corrugated pipes. Single wall corrugated pipes are lightweight, flexible, and often used for drainage and irrigation applications due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of installation. In contrast, double wall corrugated pipes feature an inner smooth wall and an outer corrugated layer, offering enhanced strength, rigidity, and flow efficiency, making them suitable for more demanding applications like stormwater management and sewage systems. Both types are valued for their durability and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
