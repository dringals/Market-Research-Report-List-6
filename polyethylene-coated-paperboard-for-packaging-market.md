<p><p>Polyethylene Coated Paperboard is utilized in packaging applications due to its moisture resistance and durability, primarily coming in two types: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is softer, flexible, and easier to form, making it suitable for applications requiring lightweight packaging. In contrast, HDPE offers superior strength and barrier properties, ideal for heavy-duty packaging needs. Both types enhance the functionality of paperboard, catering to diverse market requirements across food, beverages, and consumer goods sectors.</p></p>
