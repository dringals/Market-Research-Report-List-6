<p><p>The holographic plane gratings market is divided into two main types: transmission gratings and reflective gratings. Transmission gratings allow light to pass through, diffracting beams at specific angles, making them ideal for spectroscopy and optical communication. Reflective gratings, on the other hand, reflect light off their surfaces, enabling applications in lasers and telescopes. Both types serve diverse industries, including telecommunications, aerospace, and medical devices, leveraging their unique diffraction properties for efficient light manipulation and analysis.</p></p>
