<p><strong>The Oil-free Scroll Vacuum Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-sided Scroll</li><li>Double-sided Scroll</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil-free scroll vacuum pumps are categorized into single-sided and double-sided scroll types. Single-sided scroll pumps utilize one spiral rotor within a fixed scroll, creating vacuum through the relative motion. They are often compact and efficient for lighter applications. In contrast, double-sided scroll pumps feature two spirals, enhancing vacuum performance and capacity while maintaining low noise and vibration levels. Both types are ideal for clean environments, as they eliminate oil contamination and offer reliable, maintenance-friendly operation.</p></p>
