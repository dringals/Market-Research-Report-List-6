<p><p>The laminating epoxy resins market is classified into low viscosity and high viscosity types. Low viscosity epoxy resins are characterized by their fluid consistency, allowing for easy application and penetration into intricate molds and surfaces. They are ideal for processes like vacuum bagging and wet lay-up. In contrast, high viscosity resins have a thicker consistency, offering improved structural integrity and resistance to sagging during application. This makes them suitable for applications where dimensional stability and strength are critical.</p></p>
