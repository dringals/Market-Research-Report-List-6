<p><strong>The Automotive Door Latch Actuator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DC Motor Type</li><li>Relay Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive door latch actuator market comprises two primary types: DC motor type and relay type. The DC motor type utilizes an electric motor to operate the latch mechanism, offering precise control and quicker response times, making it popular in modern vehicles. In contrast, the relay type relies on electromagnetic relays to engage the latch, often found in older models or simpler systems due to its cost-effectiveness. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle security and convenience in door operations.</p></p>
