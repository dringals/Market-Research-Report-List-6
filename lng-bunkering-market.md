<p><strong>The LNG Bunkering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Truck-to-Ship</li><li>Ship-to-Ship</li><li>Port-to-Ship</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LNG bunkering market consists of several types based on the transfer method: </p><p>1. **Truck-to-Ship** involves transporting LNG via trucks to vessels at the dock, offering flexibility and quick delivery.</p><p>2. **Ship-to-Ship** entails transferring LNG between two vessels, usually at sea or specialized terminals, enhancing logistics and reducing port congestion.</p><p>3. **Port-to-Ship** refers to a terminal-based delivery, where LNG is supplied from a fixed facility directly to ships.</p><p>4. **Others** includes emerging methods and technologies, such as floating LNG solutions or mobile LNG units.</p></p>
