<p><p>The resistive load bank market consists of three main types: portable, trailer-mounted, and stationary load banks. Portable load banks are designed for easy transport, allowing for flexible deployment in various locations. Trailer-mounted load banks combine mobility with larger capacities, ideal for temporary or backup power applications. Stationary load banks are fixed installations used for routine testing and maintenance of generators or power systems, providing consistent performance evaluations without relocation. Each type serves specific operational needs within the energy sector.</p></p>
