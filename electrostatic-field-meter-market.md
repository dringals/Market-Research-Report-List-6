<p><p>The electrostatic field meter market is divided into two main types: non-contacting and contacting meters. Non-contacting meters measure electric fields from a distance, making them ideal for applications where direct contact is not feasible or could alter the measurement. In contrast, contacting meters require direct physical contact with the surface to measure the electrostatic charge. Each type serves specific applications in industries such as electronics, manufacturing, and safety, providing critical data for maintaining optimal electrostatic control.</p></p>
