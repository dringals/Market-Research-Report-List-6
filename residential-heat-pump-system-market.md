<p><p>The residential heat pump system market comprises three main types: electric heat pumps, gas heat pumps, and geothermal heat pumps. Electric heat pumps are popular for their efficiency and ease of installation, using electricity to transfer heat. Gas heat pumps utilize natural gas, offering a cost-effective alternative, particularly in regions with abundant gas supply. Geothermal heat pumps leverage stable underground temperatures, providing high efficiency and energy savings, making them an eco-friendly choice despite higher installation costs. Each type serves varying energy preferences and geographical conditions.</p></p>
