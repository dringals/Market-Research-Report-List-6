<p><p>The medical tray sealing machine market is divided into semi-automatic and automatic types. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention for loading and unloading trays but automate the sealing process, making them suitable for smaller operations with lower volume needs. In contrast, automatic machines handle the entire sealing process without human intervention, offering higher throughput and efficiency, making them ideal for large-scale production. Both types are essential for ensuring the integrity and sterility of medical products during packaging.</p></p>
