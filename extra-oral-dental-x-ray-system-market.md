<p><ul><li>Panoramic/Cephalometric X-Ray Unit</li><li>Cone-Beamed Computed Tomography (CBCT) System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Extraoral Dental X-Ray System market includes two primary types: Panoramic/Cephalometric X-Ray Units and Cone-Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) Systems. Panoramic units capture a wide view of the jaw, teeth, and surrounding structures in a single image, assisting in diagnostics and treatment planning. Cephalometric units focus on the lateral view of the skull for orthodontic analysis. CBCT systems provide 3D imaging, offering detailed insights into dental anatomy, enhancing precision in implant planning and surgical procedures.</p></p>
