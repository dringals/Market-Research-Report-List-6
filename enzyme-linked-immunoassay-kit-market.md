<p><p>The Enzyme-linked Immunoassay Kit market features several key types. Sandwich ELISA captures the target antigen between two antibodies, providing high specificity and sensitivity. Indirect ELISA uses a secondary antibody to amplify the signal, making it useful for detecting various analytes. Competitive ELISA involves a competition between the target antigen and a labeled antigen, suitable for small molecules. Other formats include various modifications and adaptations, catering to specific research and diagnostic needs, enhancing versatility in laboratory applications.</p></p>
