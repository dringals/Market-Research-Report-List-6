<p><p>The HER2 antibodies market comprises various targeted therapies for HER2-positive breast cancer. Trastuzumab is a monoclonal antibody that inhibits HER2 receptor signaling, while Lapatinib is a small molecule that targets HER2 and EGFR. Ado-trastuzumab emtansine combines trastuzumab with a chemotherapy drug for enhanced efficacy. Pertuzumab works alongside trastuzumab to block HER2 dimerization, improving treatment outcomes. Everolimus, an mTOR inhibitor, is used in combination with anti-HER2 therapies to address resistance and enhance effectiveness. These treatments represent significant advancements in targeted oncology.</p></p>
