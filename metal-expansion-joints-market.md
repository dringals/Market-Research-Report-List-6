<p><strong>The Metal Expansion Joints Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Expansion Joints</li><li>Angular Expansion Joints</li><li>Lateral Expansion Joints</li><li>Universal Expansion Joints</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Expansion Joints market includes various types designed to accommodate pipe movements and thermal expansion. Axial Expansion Joints allow for linear movement along the pipe's length. Angular Expansion Joints handle angular displacements while maintaining alignment. Lateral Expansion Joints accommodate side-to-side movements. Universal Expansion Joints are versatile, managing both linear and angular displacements simultaneously. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing system integrity, reducing stress, and preventing damage in piping systems across various industries.</p></p>
