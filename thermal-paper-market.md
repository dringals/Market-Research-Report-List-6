<p><strong>The Thermal Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top Coating</li><li>No Top Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal paper market is categorized into two main types based on coating: top coating and no top coating. Top-coated thermal paper features a protective layer that enhances durability, print quality, and resistance to environmental factors, making it ideal for receipts and labels exposed to moisture or abrasion. In contrast, no top coating thermal paper lacks this layer, resulting in lower cost and simpler applications, typically used for short-term printing where longevity and protection are less critical.</p></p>
