<p><p>Induction furnace transformers are essential for supplying high current at low voltage for melting and heating metals. The market is segmented into oil-filled transformers and dry-type transformers. Oil-filled transformers are known for their efficient heat dissipation and high insulation properties, making them suitable for high-capacity applications. In contrast, dry-type transformers utilize air for cooling and insulation, offering a safer, environmentally friendly option with reduced fire hazards, often preferred in urban settings or where maintenance access is limited.</p></p>
