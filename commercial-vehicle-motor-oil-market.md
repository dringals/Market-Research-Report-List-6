<p><p>The commercial vehicle motor oil market comprises primarily two types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from crude oil through refining processes, offering reliable performance but limited thermal stability and longer change intervals. In contrast, synthetic oil is chemically engineered for enhanced properties, providing superior lubrication, improved fuel efficiency, and extended engine life. As vehicles become more advanced, the demand for synthetic oil is growing, thanks to its ability to perform in extreme conditions and lower emissions.</p></p>
