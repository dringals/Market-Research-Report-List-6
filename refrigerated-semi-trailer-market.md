<p><p>The Refrigerated Semi-trailer market primarily consists of two types: Single Temperature and Multi-Temperature refrigerated semi-trailers. Single Temperature refrigerated semi-trailers maintain a consistent temperature throughout the trailer, ideal for transporting perishable goods that require uniform chilling. In contrast, Multi-Temperature refrigerated semi-trailers can maintain different temperature zones simultaneously, allowing for the transportation of diverse products with varying refrigeration needs, such as frozen foods alongside fresh produce. Both types cater to the logistics demands of temperature-sensitive cargo.</p></p>
