<p><p>The flat glass tempering furnaces market is categorized primarily into two types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal furnaces utilize a conveyor system to transport glass sheets through a heating chamber, providing uniform heating and efficient processing. In contrast, vertical furnaces allow glass to be processed in an upright position, which can optimize space and improve energy efficiency. Both types cater to different production needs, with horizontal models often preferred for larger volumes, while vertical models are advantageous for limited space and specific applications.</p></p>
