<p><p>The adhesives for the consumer electronics market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives offer flexibility and ease of application, making them ideal for bonding small components. Solid adhesives provide strong, durable bonds and are often used in assembly processes. Paste adhesives, with their thicker consistency, are suitable for gap-filling applications and offer better control during application. Each type has unique properties that cater to specific manufacturing needs in the consumer electronics industry.</p></p>
