<p><p>The SiC vacuum chucks market encompasses three primary types: SiSiC (silicon infiltrated silicon carbide), CVD SiC (chemical vapor deposited silicon carbide), and SSiC (solid-state silicon carbide). SiSiC offers enhanced toughness and thermal conductivity, making it ideal for high-performance applications. CVD SiC provides superior surface quality and precision, suitable for critical applications where accuracy is paramount. SSiC is characterized by its high hardness and resistance to wear, ideal for heavy-duty use. Each type caters to specific industrial and machining needs.</p></p>
