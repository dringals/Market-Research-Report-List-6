<p><ul><li>Single-Effect Rising Film Evaporators</li><li>Multi-Effect Rising Film Evaporators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rising Film Evaporators (RFE) are used to concentrate liquids by utilizing the rising film principle, where vaporization occurs as the liquid film ascends the evaporation surface. The market is segmented into Single-Effect and Multi-Effect Rising Film Evaporators. Single-Effect systems operate with one evaporation stage, making them simpler and cost-effective for smaller applications. Multi-Effect systems optimize energy usage by employing multiple evaporation stages, significantly enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs, making them suitable for larger industrial processes.</p></p>
