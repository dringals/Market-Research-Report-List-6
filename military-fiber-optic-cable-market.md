<p><strong>The Military Fiber Optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multimode-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military fiber optic cable market is primarily categorized into two types: single-mode and multimode cables. Single-mode cables use a single light path, allowing for long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for long-range military communications. Conversely, multimode cables contain multiple light paths, enabling higher data rates over shorter distances due to their larger core diameter. Both types are crucial for military applications, providing robust and secure communication networks in various operational environments.</p></p>
