<p><p>The Clinical Oncology Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) market comprises several key components. NGS pre-sequencing involves sample preparation and library construction to ensure accurate results. Sequencing refers to the actual process of determining the nucleotide sequences. NGS data analysis encompasses three levels: primary analysis, which involves raw data processing; secondary analysis, focusing on variant calling and annotation; and tertiary analysis, which integrates comprehensive interpretive insights for clinical decision-making. Together, these components enhance cancer diagnostics and therapeutic strategies.</p></p>
