<p><p>The Cell Culture Protein Surface Coatings market can be divided into two main types: self-coating and pre-coating. Self-coating involves using proteins that naturally attach to surfaces, promoting cell adhesion and growth without additional treatments. In contrast, pre-coating refers to surfaces that are treated in advance with specific proteins, ensuring optimal conditions for cell culture. Both types aim to enhance cell performance and viability, catering to varying requirements in research and bioproduction applications.</p></p>
