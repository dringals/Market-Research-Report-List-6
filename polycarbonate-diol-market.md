<p><p>The Polycarbonate Diol (PCD) market is segmented into two main types: Solid PCD and Liquid PCD. Solid PCD is characterized by a rigid structure, offering high thermal stability and excellent mechanical properties, making it suitable for applications in coatings, adhesives, and elastomers. Liquid PCD, on the other hand, is more flexible and easily processed, ideal for applications requiring better flow and viscosity control, such as in sealants and flexible foams. Each type caters to specific industry needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
