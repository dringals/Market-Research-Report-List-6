<p><p>The metal casting market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Gray iron castings, known for their excellent machinability and damping properties, are widely used in automotive and machinery components. Ductile iron castings, with enhanced strength and ductility, are ideal for critical applications like pipes and gears. Steel castings offer high strength and toughness, suitable for heavy-duty machinery. Aluminum castings are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, making them popular in the aerospace and automotive industries. Other castings include bronze and zinc alloys for specialized uses.</p></p>
