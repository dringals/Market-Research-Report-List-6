<p><ul><li>Top-mounted Type</li><li>Side-Mounted Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Level Float Switch market comprises two primary types: top-mounted and side-mounted switches. Top-mounted float switches are installed at the tank's top, utilizing a buoyant float that rises and falls with the liquid level, offering reliable operation for various applications. In contrast, side-mounted float switches are attached to the tank's side, providing a compact design ideal for tight spaces. Both types are widely used in industrial and residential settings for liquid level monitoring and control, catering to different installation and operational needs.</p></p>
