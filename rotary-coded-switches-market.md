<p><strong>The Rotary Coded Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMT Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary coded switches market is primarily segmented into two types: SMT (Surface Mount Technology) and Through Hole types. SMT rotary switches are compact and designed for surface mounting on printed circuit boards, offering space efficiency and enhanced performance in modern electronic devices. In contrast, Through Hole rotary switches feature leads that pass through pre-drilled holes in the PCB, providing robust mechanical stability and reliability. Both types cater to varying applications in consumer electronics, industrial equipment, and automotive systems.</p></p>
