<p><p>Non-contacting thermal mass flow meters are essential for measuring gas and liquid flow rates without direct contact with the fluid. The thermal dispersion type utilizes temperature sensors to detect heat loss from a heated element, allowing for precise flow measurements. In contrast, the boundary layer type measures the thermal characteristics of a fluid's boundary layer to determine flow rates, emphasizing minimal disruption to the flow. Both types offer reliable solutions for various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
