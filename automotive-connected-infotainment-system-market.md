<p><p>The automotive connected infotainment system market is categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Embedded Systems**: These are built directly into the vehicle's hardware, offering seamless integration and functionality like navigation and media control.</p><p>2. **Smartphone Integrated Systems**: These connect to users' smartphones, providing access to apps and features via devices like Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.</p><p>3. **Tethered Systems**: These rely on a physical connection to smartphones or other devices, allowing for data transfer and control through cables, enhancing user experience while maintaining device functionality.</p></p>
