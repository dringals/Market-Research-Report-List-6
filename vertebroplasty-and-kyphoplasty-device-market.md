<p><h1>Vertebroplasty and Kyphoplasty Device Market Dynamics 2024-2031: Also about Its Market Trends, Projections, and Opportunities</h1></p><p><strong>Vertebroplasty and Kyphoplasty Device Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>Vertebroplasty and kyphoplasty are minimally invasive procedures used to treat vertebral compression fractures, often caused by osteoporosis. Vertebroplasty involves the injection of bone cement into the fractured vertebra to stabilize it, while kyphoplasty includes inflation of a balloon before cement injection to restore vertebral height and reduce deformity. These techniques offer significant benefits, including reduced pain, improved mobility, and enhanced quality of life for patients.</p><p>The Vertebroplasty and Kyphoplasty Device Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by an aging population with increased incidences of osteoporosis-related fractures, a rise in awareness regarding these procedures, and advancements in technology that improve procedural outcomes. Additionally, an increase in healthcare expenditure and a growing preference for minimally invasive techniques contribute to market expansion. Emerging markets are also playing a pivotal role, as improved access to healthcare and innovations in surgical techniques raise the adoption of vertebroplasty and kyphoplasty.
