<p><p>Manufacturing Asset Management Systems encompass various technologies to enhance asset tracking and management. Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) uses electromagnetic fields to identify objects, facilitating inventory management. Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) provide precise tracking of assets within a facility. Barcode systems utilize printed labels scanned for inventory control, offering a cost-effective solution. Global Positioning Systems (GPS) enable tracking of assets over large distances, essential for logistics and fleet management. Together, these technologies optimize efficiency and reduce operational costs in manufacturing.</p></p>
