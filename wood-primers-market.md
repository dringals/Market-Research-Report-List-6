<p><strong>The Wood Primers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Based Primer</li><li>Water-Based Primer</li><li>Shellac-Based Primer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood primers market comprises three primary types: oil-based primers, water-based primers, and shellac-based primers. Oil-based primers offer excellent adhesion, durability, and stain-blocking properties, making them suitable for exterior and high-moisture areas. Water-based primers dry quickly and are easy to clean, making them ideal for interior applications with lower VOC emissions. Shellac-based primers provide strong stain-blocking capabilities and fast drying times, making them effective for challenging surfaces and preventing tannin bleed. Each type serves specific needs and preferences in wood finishing.</p></p>
