<p><ul><li>Single Channel Fluorometer</li><li>Multichannel Fluorometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater fluorometer market comprises two main types: single channel and multichannel fluorometers. Single channel fluorometers measure the intensity of only one fluorescent signal at a time, making them suitable for specific applications with limited parameters. In contrast, multichannel fluorometers can simultaneously measure multiple fluorescent signals across different wavelengths, allowing for more comprehensive data collection and analysis in complex aquatic environments. This capability enhances environmental monitoring, research, and resource management, catering to diverse user needs in marine science and ecology.</p></p>
