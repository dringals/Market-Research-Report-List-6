<p><ul><li>Rotary</li><li>Knife-blade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Fused Switch Disconnector market primarily comprises two types: rotary and knife-blade disconnection systems. Rotary switch disconnectors use a rotating mechanism to open or close circuits, providing a compact design suitable for various applications. In contrast, knife-blade disconnectors feature a blade that can be manually operated to isolate the circuit, commonly used in high-voltage settings due to their robust construction. Both types prioritize safety, reliability, and ease of maintenance, essential for industrial electrical installations.</p></p>
