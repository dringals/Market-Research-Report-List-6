<p><strong>The Animal Functional Proteins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrolysates</li><li>Concentrates</li><li>Isolates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal functional proteins market is segmented into hydrolysates, concentrates, and isolates. Hydrolysates are produced through enzymatic breakdown of proteins, resulting in smaller peptides that enhance digestibility and absorption, making them ideal for sports nutrition and medical applications. Concentrates retain a higher protein content while containing some fats and carbohydrates, often used in functional foods and supplements. Isolates are highly purified forms with minimal fats and carbs, offering a high protein percentage, commonly utilized in dietary products and muscle-building supplements.</p></p>
