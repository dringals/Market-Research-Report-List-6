<p><p>The semiconductor wafer polishing pad market is primarily categorized into hard Chemical Mechanical Planarization (CMP) pads and soft CMP pads. Hard CMP pads offer greater durability and are ideal for aggressive polishing applications, producing a high removal rate while maintaining surface flatness. On the other hand, soft CMP pads provide a gentler approach, suitable for delicate materials and applications that prioritize surface finish over removal rate. Each type serves distinct needs in wafer fabrication, enhancing efficiency and yield in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
