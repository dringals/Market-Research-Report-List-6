<p><ul><li>Physico-Mechanical Methods</li><li>Chemical Methods</li><li>Physico-Chemical Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fragrances microencapsulation market comprises three primary types: physico-mechanical methods, chemical methods, and physico-chemical methods. Physico-mechanical methods involve using physical processes like spray drying and coacervation to encapsulate fragrance compounds. Chemical methods rely on chemical reactions to form encapsulating materials that trap the fragrance. Physico-chemical methods combine physical and chemical processes, utilizing techniques such as sol-gel or electrospinning to achieve encapsulation. Each approach offers unique benefits in preserving fragrance stability, enhancing release properties, and improving product performance.</p></p>
