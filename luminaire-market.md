<p><p>The luminaire market is categorized into several types based on the light source technology used. LED luminaires are energy-efficient and have a long lifespan. CFL (Compact Fluorescent Lamp) luminaires are also energy-saving but have a shorter lifespan compared to LEDs. LFL (Linear Fluorescent Lamp) luminaires are commonly used in commercial settings. HID (High-Intensity Discharge) luminaires provide bright illumination, often used in outdoor applications. Halogen luminaires offer enhanced brightness and color accuracy, while incandescent luminaires are traditional but less energy-efficient.</p></p>
