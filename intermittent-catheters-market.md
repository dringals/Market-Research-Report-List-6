<p><p>Intermittent catheters are used for managing urinary retention or incontinence by allowing patients to drain urine from the bladder at regular intervals. They are a key component in urology, particularly for individuals with neurological conditions, spinal cord injuries, or other medical conditions affecting bladder control. Additionally, the growing acceptance of home healthcare solutions is further propelling the market. </p><p>The Intermittent Catheters Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.00% during the forecast period.
