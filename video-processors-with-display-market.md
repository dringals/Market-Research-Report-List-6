<p><p>Video processors with display capabilities can be categorized into two market types: single windowing and multi windowing. Single windowing processors handle one video source at a time, making them ideal for applications requiring focused content display, like surveillance or presentations. In contrast, multi windowing processors manage multiple video sources simultaneously, allowing for dynamic layouts and enhanced multitasking, commonly used in control rooms, broadcasting, and advanced video conferencing. Each type serves distinct needs based on complexity and operational requirements.</p></p>
