<p><p>The disposable surgical mask market primarily comprises three types: the three-layer non-woven mask and the four-layer non-woven mask. The three-layer mask typically includes an inner layer for moisture absorption, a middle filter layer for bacteria and particle filtration, and an outer layer for liquid resistance. The four-layer mask adds an additional protective layer, enhancing filtration efficiency and offering improved barrier protection against pathogens. Both types are essential in medical settings to reduce infection risk and ensure safety for healthcare workers and patients.</p></p>
