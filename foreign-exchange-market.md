<p><strong>The Foreign Exchange Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Currency Swaps</li><li>Outright Forward and FX Swaps</li><li>FX Options</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The foreign exchange market encompasses various instruments for currency transactions. Currency swaps involve exchanging principal and interest payments in different currencies, allowing entities to manage exposure and liquidity. Outright forwards commit to exchanging currencies at a future date at a specified rate, aiding in hedging against volatility. FX swaps combine spot and forward transactions to manage short-term liquidity needs. FX options grant the right, but not the obligation, to exchange currencies at a predetermined rate, providing flexibility and risk management.</p></p>
