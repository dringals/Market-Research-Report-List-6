<p><p>The aerosol valve market is categorized into three main types: continuous aerosol valves, metered aerosol valves, and others. Continuous aerosol valves allow for a constant release of the product until the valve is released, ideal for items like spray paints and air fresheners. Metered aerosol valves dispense a precise amount of product per actuation, making them suitable for pharmaceuticals and personal care items. The "others" category includes specialized valves for niche applications, such as automotive or industrial uses.</p></p>
