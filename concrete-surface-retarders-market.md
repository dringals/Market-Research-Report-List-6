<p><p>Concrete surface retarders are chemical agents applied to freshly poured concrete to delay the setting of the surface layer. This allows for the removal of the top layer of cement paste, exposing the aggregate beneath and creating a textured, decorative finish. These retarders are widely used in architectural and decorative concrete applications, such as driveways, patios, and building facades, to achieve aesthetically appealing surfaces.</p><p>The Concrete Surface Retarders Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period.
