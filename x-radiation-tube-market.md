<p><strong>The X-Radiation Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Anode</li><li>Rotating Anode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Radiation Tube market is primarily segmented into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode tubes. Stationary anode tubes utilize a fixed target for X-ray generation, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for lower-powered applications. In contrast, rotating anode tubes feature a rotating target, allowing for higher heat dissipation and enabling the production of higher energy X-rays. This makes them suitable for advanced imaging techniques in medical and industrial applications, where performance and efficiency are crucial.</p></p>
