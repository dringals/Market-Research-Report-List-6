<p><p>The spray skirts market consists of two primary types: nylon and neoprene spray skirts. Nylon spray skirts are lightweight, breathable, and ideal for recreational kayaking, offering water resistance and quick-drying properties. They are typically more affordable but less durable in harsh conditions. Neoprene spray skirts, on the other hand, provide superior water resistance and insulation, making them suitable for cold-weather kayaking and more challenging environments. These skirts are thicker and more robust, catering to serious kayakers seeking performance and durability.</p></p>
