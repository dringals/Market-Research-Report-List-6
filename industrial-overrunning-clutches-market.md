<p><ul><li>Sprag Clutch</li><li>Spring Clutches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial overrunning clutches market encompasses key types such as sprag clutches and spring clutches. Sprag clutches allow for one-way motion by engaging automatically upon load application, making them ideal for applications requiring unidirectional rotation. Spring clutches, on the other hand, utilize a coil spring mechanism to engage and disengage with torque variations, providing flexibility in control. Both types ensure efficient power transfer and prevent backspin, catering to diverse industrial applications like conveyors, elevators, and automotive systems.</p></p>
