<p><strong>The Perovskite Photovoltaics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Structure</li><li>Inverted Structure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perovskite photovoltaics are classified into two main market types: normal structure and inverted structure. The normal structure typically features a substrate layer, with the perovskite layer positioned atop a hole transport layer, facilitating efficient light absorption and charge transport. In contrast, the inverted structure reverses this arrangement, placing the perovskite layer between an electron transport layer and a transparent conductor, enhancing stability and reducing defects. Both structures aim to optimize efficiency and scalability for commercial solar energy applications.</p></p>
