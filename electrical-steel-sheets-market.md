<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical steel sheets market is primarily divided into two types: oriented electrical steel (OE) and non-oriented electrical steel (NOE). Oriented electrical steel features a specific grain orientation that enhances magnetic properties, making it ideal for applications like transformers and electric motors, where efficiency is critical. In contrast, non-oriented electrical steel has a uniform grain structure, providing versatile performance in various applications, including generators and motors, where cost-effectiveness and easier manufacturing are prioritized. Both types are essential for efficient electrical machinery.</p></p>
