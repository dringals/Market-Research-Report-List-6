<p><p>The liquid filtration market comprises various types based on material and construction. Monofilament polypropylene fabrics provide chemical resistance with uniform pore size for consistent performance. Multifilament polypropylene fabrics, made of multiple filaments, offer enhanced filtration capabilities and flexibility. Nonwoven polypropylene felt is suitable for high dirt holding capacity, while nonwoven polyester felt is known for its strength and temperature resistance, catering to diverse filtration needs.</p></p>
