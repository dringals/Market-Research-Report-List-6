<p><p>The Automotive Hands-Free Liftgate market is divided into two main types: fully automatic and semi-automatic systems. Fully automatic liftgates allow users to open or close the trunk by simply waving a foot under the rear bumper, without any manual effort. In contrast, semi-automatic liftgates require a slight user input to initiate the opening or closing process, often needing a button press or a light kick. Both types enhance convenience and accessibility, especially when hands are full.</p></p>
