<p><p>The urology lasers market comprises primarily solid-state lasers and gas-fired lasers. Solid-state lasers utilize a solid medium, often crystals or glass, to produce high-energy beams suitable for surgical procedures, offering precision and reduced tissue damage. In contrast, gas-fired lasers generate energy through the excitation of gas molecules, providing consistent performance and versatility in various applications. Both types enhance surgical outcomes in urology by enabling minimally invasive procedures and improving recovery times for patients.</p></p>
