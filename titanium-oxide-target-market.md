<p><p>The titanium oxide target market comprises two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, stationary surfaces used in various deposition processes, offering uniform coating over a limited area. In contrast, rotating targets incorporate a cylindrical design that allows for continuous surface exposure and better utilization of coating material, resulting in enhanced deposition efficiency. Both types serve critical roles in applications like semiconductor manufacturing and thin-film coatings, catering to diverse industrial needs and technologically advanced processes.</p></p>
