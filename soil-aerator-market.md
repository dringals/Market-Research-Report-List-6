<p><strong>The Soil Aerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil aerator market comprises two primary types: mechanical and pneumatic aerators. Mechanical aerators use physical tools, such as spikes or blades, to penetrate the soil, creating holes for air, water, and nutrients to reach the roots. They are ideal for small to medium-sized areas. Pneumatic aerators, on the other hand, utilize compressed air to create voids in the soil, promoting deeper aeration. This type is often used for larger landscapes, offering efficient and effective soil enhancement for better plant health.</p></p>
