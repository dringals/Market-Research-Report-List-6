<p><strong>The Advanced HDI PCB Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDI PCB (1+N+1)</li><li>HDI PCB (2+N+2)</li><li>ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Advanced HDI PCB market primarily consists of various types catering to different technological needs. The HDI PCB (1+N+1) features one buried layer and one via layer, offering enhanced density and performance. The HDI PCB (2+N+2) includes two stacked microvias and additional layers, enabling even greater complexity and functionality. ELIC (Every Layer Interconnection) PCBs allow connections across all layers, facilitating intricate designs for high-performance applications. These types cater to the demand for compact and efficient electronic devices across various industries.</p></p>
