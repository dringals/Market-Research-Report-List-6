<p><p>The drum drying equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: single drum and double drum drying equipment. Single drum dryers consist of a single heating drum where products are placed and dried, suitable for smaller batches and specific applications. In contrast, double drum dryers feature two adjacent drums, allowing for higher capacity and efficient heat transfer, making them ideal for large-scale production. Both types are utilized in various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals, to achieve effective moisture removal.</p></p>
