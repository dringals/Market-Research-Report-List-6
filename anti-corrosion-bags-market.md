<p><p>The anti-corrosion bags market is primarily segmented into mono-layer and multi-layer types. Mono-layer bags consist of a single polymer layer that provides basic protection against moisture and corrosion, making them cost-effective for short-term storage. Conversely, multi-layer bags combine several barrier materials, enhancing protection against gases, moisture, and corrosive agents, suitable for long-term storage applications. Multi-layer bags are often used in industries like aerospace and electronics, which require superior corrosion protection.</p></p>
