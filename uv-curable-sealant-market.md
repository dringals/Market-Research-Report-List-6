<p><p>The UV Curable Sealant market is divided into two main types: rigid and flexible sealants. Rigid sealants are designed to provide strong, inflexible bonds, making them ideal for applications that require durability and resistance to harsh conditions. In contrast, flexible sealants offer the ability to accommodate movement and expansion, making them suitable for areas prone to vibration or thermal changes. Both types benefit from UV curing technology, which enhances process efficiency and ensures strong adherence to various substrates.</p></p>
