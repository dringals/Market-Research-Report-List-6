<p><ul><li>Hard Coking Coals (HCC)</li><li>Medium Coking Coal</li><li>Semi-soft Coking Coal (SSCC)</li><li>Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallurgical coal market comprises several types essential for steel production. Hard Coking Coal (HCC) is the highest quality, delivering superior carbon content and strength for metallurgical coke. Medium Coking Coal offers slightly lower quality but remains valuable for coking processes. Semi-soft Coking Coal (SSCC) contains characteristics suited for blending, enhancing coke production. Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal is used to supplement coke in blast furnaces, improving efficiency and reducing costs, making it increasingly popular in steelmaking.</p></p>
