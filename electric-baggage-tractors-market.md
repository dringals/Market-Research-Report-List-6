Lead-acid batteries are traditional, cost-effective solutions with a robust lifecycle but heavier weight and longer charging times. In contrast, Li-Ion batteries offer superior energy density, faster charging, lighter weight, and longer lifespans, making them increasingly popular for modern electric baggage tractors. Each battery type presents distinct advantages and challenges, influencing their adoption rates in airport operations and logistics sectors.</p></p>
