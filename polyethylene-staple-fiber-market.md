<p><p>Polyethylene Staple Fiber (PSF) is classified into three main market types based on density: Low Density (LD), Medium Density (MD), and High Density (HD). LD PSF is flexible and lightweight, ideal for applications requiring softness, such as in textiles and stuffing. MD PSF strikes a balance between flexibility and strength, making it suitable for versatile applications like non-wovens. HD PSF is robust and rigid, often used in industries demanding durability, such as automotive and construction. Each type serves distinct end-use needs.</p></p>
