<p><p>The ultraviolet (UV) LED technology market is divided into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA LEDs emit long wavelengths (315-400 nm), primarily used in curing processes and skin treatments. UVB LEDs have a medium wavelength range (280-315 nm), utilized in phototherapy and applications like pest control. UVC LEDs, with short wavelengths (100-280 nm), are effective for sterilization and disinfection, particularly in water treatment and air purification. Each type serves unique applications across various industries.</p></p>
