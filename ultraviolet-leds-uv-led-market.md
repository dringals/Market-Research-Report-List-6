<p><p>Ultraviolet LEDs (UV-LEDs) are categorized into several types based on their wavelength ranges and applications. UV-B LEDs, emitting 280-320 nm, are primarily utilized in phototherapy and skin treatment. UV-C LEDs, ranging from 200-280 nm, are effective for sterilization and disinfection.
