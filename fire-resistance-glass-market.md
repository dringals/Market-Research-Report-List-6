<p><p>Fire resistance glass is categorized into three main types based on heat insulation properties. </p><p>1. **Heat Insulation**: This type effectively minimizes heat transfer during a fire, maintaining lower temperatures on the non-fire side and enhancing safety.</p><p>2. **No Heat Insulation**: This glass allows heat to pass through, providing visibility but limited heat protection, typically used in applications where fire resistance is prioritized over insulation.</p><p>3. **Part of the Heat Insulation Market**: This segment includes products that strike a balance, offering some level of thermal resistance while maintaining structural integrity during a fire emergency.</p></p>
