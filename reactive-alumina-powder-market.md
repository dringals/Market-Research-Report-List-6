<p><p>The Reactive Alumina Powder market is categorized primarily into two types: powdered form and sphered form reactive alumina. Powdered form reactive alumina is characterized by its fine particle size, offering high surface area and reactivity, making it suitable for applications like catalysts and adsorbents. In contrast, sphered form reactive alumina features uniform spherical particles, enhancing its flow properties and reducing dust generation. This form is ideal for applications in ceramics, pharmaceuticals, and advanced materials, where consistency and performance are crucial.</p></p>
