<p><p>The Smart Grid T&D (Transmission and Distribution) Equipment market is divided into two primary categories: Transmission Equipment and Distribution Equipment. Transmission Equipment includes high-voltage systems, transformers, and switchgear that facilitate the long-distance movement of electricity from power plants to substations. Distribution Equipment focuses on low-voltage infrastructure such as transformers, circuit breakers, and smart meters that deliver electricity from substations to consumers. Both segments aim to enhance reliability, efficiency, and integration of renewable energy sources within the grid.</p></p>
