<p><p>The AEB (Autonomous Emergency Braking) system market includes three main types: Forward Collision Warning, Dynamic Brake Support, and Crash Imminent Braking. Forward Collision Warning alerts drivers of potential collisions, enhancing situational awareness. Dynamic Brake Support provides automatic braking assistance to reduce impact severity. Crash Imminent Braking autonomously activates brakes when a collision is unavoidable. Together, these technologies aim to improve vehicle safety, reduce accident rates, and advance autonomous driving capabilities by mitigating collision risks effectively.</p></p>
