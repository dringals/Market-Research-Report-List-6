<p><p>The bunker oil market encompasses various fuel types primarily for marine vessels. Residual fuel, often referred to as heavy fuel oil (HFO), is the leftover product from crude oil refining, characterized by its higher viscosity and lower cost, making it suitable for larger ships. In contrast, distillate fuel, which includes marine gas oil (MGO) and marine diesel oil (MDO), is refined to a lighter, cleaner standard with lower sulfur content, catering to vessels requiring higher efficiency and environmental compliance.</p></p>
