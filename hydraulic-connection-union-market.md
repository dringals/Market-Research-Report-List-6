<p><p>The hydraulic connection union market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Welded Type**: These unions are permanently fused together, providing a strong, leak-proof joint ideal for high-pressure systems.</p><p>2. **Card Sleeve Type**: This design features a sleeve that securely holds the components together, allowing for quick assembly and disassembly.</p><p>3. **Withhold Type**: This type includes mechanisms that hold the parts firmly, ensuring a reliable connection under pressure while allowing maintenance access.</p><p>4. **Flaring Type**: These unions create a flared connection, enhancing sealing performance and resistance to vibration in hydraulic applications.</p></p>
