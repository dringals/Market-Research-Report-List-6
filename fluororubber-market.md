<p><strong>The Fluororubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FKM</li><li>FSR</li><li>FFKM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluororubber market comprises three main types: FKM, FSR, and FFKM. FKM (fluoroelastomer) is widely used for its excellent resistance to heat, chemicals, and oil, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. FSR (fluorosilicone rubber) combines the properties of silicone and fluorocarbon, offering resistance to fuels and oils, ideal for aerospace and automotive sectors. FFKM (perfluoroelastomer) provides exceptional chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making it essential for demanding environments like pharmaceuticals and semiconductor manufacturing. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
