<p><p>The touchless car wash machines market mainly consists of two types: tunnel-type and rollover-type. Tunnel-type systems operate with vehicles moving through a lengthy wash tunnel, where various cleaning components and high-pressure water jets clean the car thoroughly. Rollover-type machines feature a stationary wash unit that rolls over the vehicle, applying water and soap while gently scrubbing. Both types ensure minimal contact with the car's surface, reducing the risk of scratches and damage while providing effective cleaning solutions.</p></p>
