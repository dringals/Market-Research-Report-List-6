<p><p>The dewatering pumps market consists of two main types: submersible and non-submersible dewatering pumps. Submersible dewatering pumps are designed to operate while submerged in water, efficiently removing fluids from flooded areas or construction sites. In contrast, non-submersible pumps, typically placed above the water level, use suction to draw water from a lower reservoir. Both types are crucial for various applications, including mining, construction, and municipal projects, ensuring effective water management and site safety.</p></p>
