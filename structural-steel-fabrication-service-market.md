<p><p>The structural steel fabrication service market encompasses various steel shapes essential for construction and infrastructure. I-beams are favored for their high strength-to-weight ratio, ideal for beams and columns. Angle shapes (L-shape) are versatile in framing and bracing. Hollow Structural Sections (HSS) provide aesthetic appeal and structural integrity, often used in modern designs. Z-shaped sections are utilized for effective load distribution in roofing and bracing, while T-shaped beams offer support in applications requiring a flat surface for connections.</p></p>
