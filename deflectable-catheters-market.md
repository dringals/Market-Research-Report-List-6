<p><strong>The Deflectable Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unidirectional Deflectable</li><li>Didirectional Deflectable</li><li>Multidirectional Deflectable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Deflectable catheters are categorized based on their bending capabilities. Unidirectional deflectable catheters can bend in one direction, allowing for targeted access in procedures that require precision. Bidirectional deflectable catheters provide flexibility to bend in two opposing directions, enhancing maneuverability and navigation through complex anatomy. Multidirectional deflectable catheters offer the most versatility, enabling multiple angles of deflection, which is advantageous for intricate interventions. Each type enhances procedural effectiveness by improving access and control during minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
