<p><p>Thermochromic inks are temperature-sensitive materials that change color in response to varying heat levels. Cold activated thermochromic inks react to cooler temperatures, becoming visible when exposed to cold surfaces, often used in novelty items and packaging. Touch activated inks change color upon contact, commonly found in interactive products and educational tools. Warm activated thermochromic inks respond to elevated temperatures, ideal for applications like temperature indicators in food packaging, ensuring safety and functionality through visual cues.</p></p>
