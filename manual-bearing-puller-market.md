<p><p>A Manual Bearing Puller is a specialized tool designed for the removal of bearings, gears, and other round components from shafts or housings without damaging the parts. The device typically features two or three arms that grip the bearing, while a central screw mechanism is employed to push against the shaft, allowing for smooth extraction. Increasing demand for precision tools in various industrial sectors such as automotive, manufacturing, and construction is driving growth in the manual bearing puller market.</p><p>The Manual Bearing Puller Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.00% during the forecast period.
