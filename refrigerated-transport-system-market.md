<p><p>The refrigerated transport system market is primarily divided into two types: chilled and frozen. Chilled transport maintains temperatures typically between 0°C to 5°C, preserving perishable goods such as fruits, vegetables, dairy, and certain meats during transit. In contrast, frozen transport operates at temperatures below -18°C, ensuring the long-term preservation of items like ice cream, frozen seafood, and other deep-frozen products. Both sectors are crucial for maintaining food quality and safety throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
