<p><p>PE-RT (Polyethylene of Raised Temperature) oxygen barrier pipes are classified into two main types: PE-RT I and PE-RT II. PE-RT I pipes are designed for residential applications, offering moderate temperature resistance and flexibility, making them suitable for heating systems and plumbing. In contrast, PE-RT II pipes provide enhanced performance for industrial and high-demand applications, featuring improved thermal stability and pressure resistance. Both types effectively prevent oxygen diffusion, prolonging the lifespan of heating and plumbing systems by reducing corrosion in metal components.</p></p>
