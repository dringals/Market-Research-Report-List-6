<p><p>Real-Time Location Systems (RTLS) enable the tracking of assets and individuals in real time within specified environments. The market comprises several technologies: RFID uses electromagnetic fields to identify tags attached to objects. Wi-Fi leverages existing wireless networks for location data. Ultrasound employs sound waves for proximity sensing. Infrared utilizes light pulses to track items. Zigbee provides low-power, short-range communication ideal for IoT applications. Ultra Wide Band (UWB) offers precise location accuracy for real-time tracking in complex environments.</p></p>
