<p><p>Ground Support Equipment (GSE) tires are categorized into solid tires and pneumatic tires, each serving specific operational needs. Solid tires are made from durable materials, providing puncture resistance and low maintenance, making them ideal for heavy equipment in confined spaces. In contrast, pneumatic tires are air-filled, offering better shock absorption and traction on varied surfaces, suitable for versatile outdoor applications. The choice between these tire types depends on factors like operational environment, load capacity, and maintenance preferences.</p></p>
