<p><p>The Immuno-Oncology market encompasses various therapeutic approaches aimed at harnessing the body's immune system to combat cancer. Immune checkpoint inhibitors block proteins that suppress immune responses, enhancing anti-tumor activity. Cytokine-based immunotherapies use signaling proteins to stimulate immune cells. Cancer vaccines aim to provoke an immune response against specific cancer antigens. CAR-T cell therapy involves genetically modifying a patient’s T cells to target cancer cells more effectively. Other therapies include oncolytic viruses and monoclonal antibodies, contributing to diverse treatment options.</p></p>
