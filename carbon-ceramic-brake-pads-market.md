<p><p>The Carbon Ceramic Brake Pads market features two main types: Carbon Fiber Ceramic and Carbon Fiber Cermet. Carbon Fiber Ceramic pads integrate carbon fibers with ceramic materials, offering excellent heat resistance, durability, and lightweight characteristics, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. On the other hand, Carbon Fiber Cermet pads combine carbon fibers with metallic compounds, providing enhanced friction properties and improved wear resistance, catering to applications where both performance and longevity are essential. Both types are gaining traction in premium automotive sectors.</p></p>
