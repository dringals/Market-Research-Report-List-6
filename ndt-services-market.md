<p><p>NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) services encompass various methods to evaluate materials without causing damage. Ultrasonic Testing utilizes sound waves to detect imperfections within materials. Radiography Testing employs X-rays to visualize internal structures. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials using magnetic fields and particles. Liquid Penetrant Testing involves applying a liquid dye to reveal surface flaws. Other methods may include eddy current testing and visual inspection, broadening the scope of NDT applications across industries.</p></p>
