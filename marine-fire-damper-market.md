<p><p>The marine fire damper market can be categorized into two main types based on the actuators used. Electrical actuator types are typically automated, using electric signals to open or close dampers, ensuring quick and reliable response during fire events. In contrast, manual actuator types require human intervention for operation, often involving lever or handwheel mechanisms. Both types are essential for controlling smoke and heat in marine environments, enhancing safety by regulating airflow during emergencies.</p></p>
