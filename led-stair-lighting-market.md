<p><strong>The LED Stair Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LED Step Light</li><li>LED Strip Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED stair lighting market primarily features two types: LED step lights and LED strip lights. LED step lights are designed to illuminate individual steps, enhancing safety and aesthetics with focused lighting. They are often integrated into the risers or mounted on walls. In contrast, LED strip lights provide flexible illumination along the edges of stairs, creating a continuous light effect that can be customized for ambiance. Both options enhance visibility, reduce accidents, and elevate the overall design of staircases.</p></p>
