PPDs are widely used for their excellent heat resistance and aging properties, making them essential in tire production. RD (TMQ) is favored for its high efficiency in extending the lifespan of rubber products under harsh conditions. Other types may include various proprietary formulations and secondary antioxidants that enhance performance and stability in diverse rubber applications, such as automotive and industrial uses.</p></p>
