<p><p>The water-based fertilizer colorants market primarily consists of two types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are soluble colorants that impart vibrant hues to fertilizers, enhancing their visual appeal and helping in product differentiation. Conversely, pigments are insoluble particles that are mixed into fertilizers, providing stability and long-lasting color without leaching. Both types serve the functional purpose of improving the aesthetic quality of fertilizers, making them more attractive to consumers while also assisting in brand recognition in the competitive agricultural market.</p></p>
