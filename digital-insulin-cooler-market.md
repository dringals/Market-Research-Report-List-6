<p><p>The digital insulin cooler market comprises two main types: powered insulin coolers and non-powered insulin coolers. In contrast, non-powered insulin coolers rely on passive insulation methods to keep insulin cool without any electrical components, making them portable and convenient for users on the go, albeit with limitations in temperature control consistency.</p></p>
