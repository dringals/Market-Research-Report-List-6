<p><p>The Stationary Emission Control Catalyst (SECC) is a technology used to reduce harmful emissions from stationary sources such as power plants, industrial facilities, and chemical plants. These catalysts convert pollutants like nitrogen oxides (NOx), carbon monoxide (CO), and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) into less harmful substances such as nitrogen, carbon dioxide, and water vapor. The increasing focus on environmental regulations and the need for sustainable industrial practices are driving the demand for SECC technologies.</p><p>The Stationary Emission Control Catalyst Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.5% during the forecast period.
