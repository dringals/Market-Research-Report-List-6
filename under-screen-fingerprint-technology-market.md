<p><strong>The Under-screen Fingerprint Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Fingerprint Recognition</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Recognition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Under-screen fingerprint technology primarily includes two market types: optical fingerprint recognition and ultrasonic fingerprint recognition. Optical fingerprint recognition uses light-based sensors to capture a fingerprint image through the screen, making it cost-effective and widely adopted in smartphones. Conversely, ultrasonic fingerprint recognition employs high-frequency sound waves to create a three-dimensional map of the fingerprint, offering enhanced security and accuracy, even on wet or oily fingers. Both technologies enable seamless user authentication while maximizing screen real estate in devices.</p></p>
