<p><p>The Polar Satcom market is classified into three types based on orbital altitudes: Low Polar Orbit (LPO), Middle Polar Orbit (MPO), and High Polar Orbit (HPO). LPO satellites, typically below 2,000 kilometers, offer low latency and high-resolution data for applications like Earth monitoring. MPO, ranging from 2,000 to 30,000 kilometers, balances coverage and data transmission. HPO satellites, above 30,000 kilometers, provide extensive coverage for global communication but with increased latency, making them suitable for remote connectivity and broadcasting services.</p></p>
