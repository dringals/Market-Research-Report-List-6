<p><p>Bare copper tape is available in three main types based on hardness: annealed (soft), half-hard, and hard. Annealed copper tape is highly malleable, making it ideal for applications requiring intricate bending and shaping. Half-hard tape offers a balance of flexibility and strength, suitable for general use where moderate rigidity is desired. Hard copper tape is the least malleable, providing enhanced durability and support in high-stress environments. Each type caters to specific applications based on the required mechanical properties.</p></p>
