Warfarin, an oral vitamin K antagonist, requires regular monitoring due to its interactions. Direct oral anticoagulants (DOACs) like Apixaban, Dabigatran, Rivaroxaban, and Edoxaban offer convenient dosing with fewer dietary restrictions and no routine monitoring. These medications significantly enhance VTE management by preventing clot formation and reducing associated complications.</p></p>
