<p><p>The LED Protection Devices market includes several key types designed to safeguard LED systems. Shunt Protection Devices redirect excess current during faults, preventing damage to LEDs. Surge Protective Devices mitigate voltage spikes from surges, enhancing longevity. Bypass Protection Devices allow uninterrupted operation by bypassing faulty LEDs without disrupting the circuit. Other market types may include thermal protection and overvoltage protection devices, which further enhance reliability and safety in LED applications, ensuring optimal performance and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
