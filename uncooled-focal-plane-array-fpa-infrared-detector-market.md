<p><p>The Uncooled Focal Plane Array (FPA) infrared detector market primarily features two types: Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx detectors utilize vanadium oxide as a sensing material, offering high sensitivity and excellent thermal stability, making them suitable for applications like surveillance and automotive safety. In contrast, A-Si detectors employ amorphous silicon technology, providing cost-effective solutions with good performance for various applications, including industrial and consumer electronics. Both types cater to diverse infrared imaging needs.</p></p>
